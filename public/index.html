Discover the Wild & Wonderful World of MixZoo

From friendly domestic companions to majestic forest adventurers and mysterious night-time flyers, MixZoo brings together a diverse cast of creatures in one playful, educational universe. Meet the animals that make our brand unforgettable.

Friendly Companions

Loyal Paws & Purrs

Dogs, cats, and the pets that feel like family. These cuddly characters bring warmth, loyalty, and a whole lot of tail-wagging fun to the MixZoo crew.

Underwater Explorers

Deep Blue Discoveries

Dive into the deep with our shark fish, colorful coral reef fish, and the tiny otocinclus catfish. These aquatic adventurers prove that life under the sea is full of surprises.

Forest Adventurers

Wild Woods Wanderers

Bears, monkeys, and the curious creatures of the canopy. These forest friends bring a sense of adventure, teaching us about the magic of the great outdoors.

Night-Time Flyers

Moonlight Mysteries

When the sun sets, the bats and wolves come out to play. Discover the fascinating world of different types of bats and the loyal pack spirit of the wolf under the stars.

Why Families & School Groups Love MixZoo

We bring the wild, wonderful world of animals directly to your community with an educational, hands-on experience that sparks curiosity in every child. From fascinating types of bats to vibrant coral reef fish, our diverse animal cast creates unforgettable memories.

Animal Cast

Unforgettable Animal Diversity

From the loyal dog to the mysterious bat, our eclectic MixZoo crew features eight unique species. Kids get to meet a shark fish, a playful monkey, and even a wolf, learning about habitats and behaviors in a safe, engaging environment.

Educational

School-Friendly Storytelling

Our programs are designed with educators in mind. We weave curriculum-aligned lessons into every visit, covering biology, conservation, and the unique traits of creatures like the otocinclus catfish and coral reef fish. It's learning disguised as pure fun.

Team

Approachable Team-Led Experiences

Led by CEO Michael Buell, the dedicated MixZoo team brings passion and expertise to every interaction. We pride ourselves on creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where every child feels comfortable asking questions and exploring the animal kingdom up close.
