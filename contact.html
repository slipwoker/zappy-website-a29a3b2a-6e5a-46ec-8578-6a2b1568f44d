Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the MixZoo experience, our animal cast, and how to connect with CEO Michael Buell and the team.

Who is MixZoo for?

MixZoo is built for families, school groups, and community organizations looking for a unique animal entertainment experience. Whether you love dogs, cats, or are fascinated by creatures like bats and sharks, our diverse cast of domestic and wild animal characters creates a welcoming adventure for all ages.

What kinds of groups can contact the team?

We work with educational groups, birthday parties, corporate events, and community festivals. Our animal ambassadors — from playful monkeys to majestic wolves — are trained to deliver engaging, safe, and memorable presentations tailored to your group's interests.

How are the animal characters presented?

Each character is introduced with a storybook-style narrative that highlights their personality and natural habitat. From coral reef fish to the mysterious types of bats, every animal is celebrated through educational storytelling that sparks curiosity and respect for wildlife.

How can I reach Michael Buell or the MixZoo team?

The best way to connect with CEO Michael Buell and the dedicated MixZoo team is through our main contact page. Simply fill out the contact form with your inquiry, and we'll get back to you promptly to discuss bookings, partnerships, or any questions you may have.
