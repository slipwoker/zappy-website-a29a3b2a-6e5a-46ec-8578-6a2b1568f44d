A Wildly Different Kind of Story

At MixZoo, we believe that animal entertainment should be more than just a spectacle—it should be a warm, welcoming adventure for everyone. From the loyal dog to the soaring bat, the curious monkey to the mysterious shark, our eclectic cast of creatures brings together the best of the wild and the wonderful.

02

Rooted in Community, Led by Passion

Founded by CEO Michael Buell, MixZoo was built on a simple idea: create a space where families, school groups, and pet lovers can connect with animals in a way that feels personal and memorable. Our dedicated MixZoo team works every day to transform curiosity into connection, whether you're fascinated by coral reef fish or captivated by the silent flight of different types of bats.

03

Our Mission: Make Every Visit Unforgettable

We're on a mission to make animal entertainment approachable, educational, and bursting with personality. Through playful storytelling, cozy environments, and a deep respect for every creature—from the tiny otocinclus catfish to the mighty wolf—MixZoo creates moments that stay with you long after you leave.

Meet Our Founder & CEO,
Michael Buell

Michael Buell leads MixZoo with a vision that blends wild imagination with thoughtful care. His passion for animals — from loyal dogs and curious cats to fascinating creatures like bats and coral reef fish — sparked the idea of a brand where every creature has a story to tell. Michael believes that learning about animals should feel like an adventure, not a lecture.

Under his leadership, MixZoo has grown into a vibrant community hub where families, school groups, and pet lovers discover the joy of the animal kingdom. His approach is grounded in three core values that shape everything we do.

Creature-First Care

Community Adventure

Organized Playfulness

Our Core Values

These are the guiding principles that shape every experience at MixZoo — from our animal family to our community.

Curiosity

We believe that every creature, from the smallest otocinclus catfish to the largest shark fish, has a story worth discovering. Our programs spark wonder and encourage lifelong learning about the animal kingdom.

Kindness

Compassion is at the heart of MixZoo. We treat every animal — whether a playful dog, a curious bat, or a majestic wolf — with the respect and gentle care they deserve, creating a safe haven for all.

Adventure

From the depths of coral reef fish habitats to the mysterious world of different types of bats, we bring the thrill of exploration to every visit. Adventure is our compass, guiding us to create unforgettable encounters.

Community

MixZoo is more than a collection of animals — it's a family. Led by CEO Michael Buell and our passionate MixZoo team, we build connections between people and wildlife, fostering a sense of belonging for every visitor.

Where curious creatures and happy humans meet — MixZoo brings animal adventures to life with playful experiences, educational shows, and a whole lot of heart.
