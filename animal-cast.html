Meet the MixZoo Cast

Every creature brings a spark of personality to our wild, wonderful crew. From loyal paws to curious fins, each animal adds a unique note to the MixZoo adventure — a place where domestic charm meets untamed spirit.

Fan Favorites

Dog & Cat

The heart of the MixZoo home. Our loyal dog brings boundless energy and comforting warmth, while the curious cat adds a touch of independent charm and quiet mischief. Together they remind us that the best adventures start at home.

Aquatic

Fish & Shark

Dive into the deep end with our dazzling coral reef fish and the ever-majestic shark. They glide through the MixZoo waters with grace and a hint of mystery, showing us that every ecosystem has its gentle giants and tiny treasures.

Woodland

Bear & Monkey

From the forest canopy to the berry-filled clearings, our bear and monkey duo bring laughter and strength. The bear’s gentle wisdom pairs perfectly with the monkey’s acrobatic antics, making every woodland tale unforgettable.

Nocturnal

Bat & Wolf

When the sun sets, our night crew takes the stage. The bat navigates the twilight skies with astonishing precision — a reminder of the many fascinating types of bats — while the wolf’s loyal howl echoes across the moonlit hills, calling the pack together.

Led by CEO Michael Buell and the passionate MixZoo team, every animal adventure is crafted with care, creativity, and a deep love for creatures great and small.
